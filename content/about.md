I am Isaac Yong, a second-year MEng Computing student at Imperial College London, specialising in Visual Computing and Robotics.

Right now, a lot of my time goes into building software for robotics and education.

I am the Software Lead of First Order Robotics, where I lead the software team for the RoboCup Small Size League competition. That means working on systems that have to be practical, reliable, and fast enough to perform under real constraints.

I also built SPM Chat, a free chatbot for Malaysian students. I care a lot about making useful tools more accessible, especially when they can lower the barrier for learning.

This website is my corner of the internet for sharing:

- project updates
- technical notes
- ideas I am still figuring out
- occasional reflections on learning, robotics, and building things
